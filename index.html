About the game

Pixel Invaders is a fast-paced arcade-style space shooter where players survive endless waves of enemies and compete for high scores.

Frequently asked questions

The game crashes or won’t open

Try fully closing the app and reopening it. If the problem continues, restart your device and ensure your system is up to date. If it still fails, please contact us using the email below and include your device model and operating system version so we can help.

How do I report a bug?

Send an email to our support address with a short description of what happened, what you were doing in the game when it occurred, and your device and OS version. Screenshots or screen recordings are welcome if they help illustrate the issue.

Suggestions or feedback

We appreciate hearing from players. If you have ideas for improvements or general feedback, email us anytime. Your message helps shape future updates.
